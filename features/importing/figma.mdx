---
title: "Import from Figma"
description: "Import Figma designs into Kommit to analyze screens and component structure."
---

Import your Figma designs to help Kommit understand your product's visual structure. Kommit analyzes the screens and component hierarchy to inform your product spec.

## Steps

1. Go to **Import** in the sidebar and select **Figma Design**
2. Paste your Figma file URL (e.g., `https://www.figma.com/design/...`)
3. Click **Import**

## What gets analyzed

- **Screens and pages** — Individual frames and their hierarchy
- **Component structure** — Reusable components and variants

## What's not included

- Prototype interactions and transitions
- Comments and annotations
- Version history

<Note>
  Figma import analyzes the design structure to enrich your spec. It does not generate code from designs — use the canvas conversation to discuss implementation approaches.
</Note>
